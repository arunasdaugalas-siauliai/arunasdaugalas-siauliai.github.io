About the company

UAB Odarūna is a Lithuanian company operating since 2004. We maintain a stable supply network, clear quality standards and reliable partners both in Lithuania and abroad.

We purchase and sell cattle hides – fresh (chilled), wet‑salted and wet‑blue grades. Flexible on quantities, sorting and packaging; we work to buyer specifications or our own standards.

  • Timely payments and transparent logistics (road & sea via Klaipėda port).
  • EU documentation and quality control at every stage.
  • Long‑term partnerships – prioritising stability and reliability.
